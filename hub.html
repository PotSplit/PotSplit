Get Started with PotSplit Tools

We make it easy for individuals to create compliant contribution pages for celebrations, team goals, and more.

📝 How to Structure Your Page

  • ✅ Always offer free participation – no purchase is ever required
  • ✅ Clearly explain what contributions support (e.g., a gift or thank-you fund)
  • ✅ No random winners, raffles, lotteries, or prize-based contests

💳 Stripe & Legal Guidelines

Use Stripe for collecting optional contributions. We recommend:

  • Using Stripe Payment Links
  • Including our “No Purchase Necessary” disclaimer
  • Linking to your personal Terms of Use and Privacy Policy
